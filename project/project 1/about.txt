Line Following Robot
Overview
Successfully designed and engineered an autonomous line-following robot to compete in the University Robotics Competition. This project focused on creating a high-speed, stable, and precise robot capable of navigating complex tracks with sharp turns and intersections using advanced sensor fusion and control logic.

Technical Specifications & Features
Precision Navigation: Integrated a high-density Infrared (IR) sensor array to detect path deviations with millisecond accuracy.

Advanced Control Logic: Implemented a PID (Proportional-Integral-Derivative) Control Algorithm to ensure smooth movements, prevent overshooting, and maintain high speeds during straight paths.

Power & Drive System: Optimized with high-RPM geared DC motors and a dedicated motor driver (e.g., L298N/TB6612FNG) for rapid response times.

Robust Calibration: Developed a custom calibration routine to adapt to varying lighting conditions and surface reflections on the competition track.
